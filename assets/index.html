About Us

Dequinox 2018 , the cultural festival of JUET Guna is widely renowned as the largest fest in central India. With a participation roster exceeding over 1 hundred colleges across the academic demographic of India, Dequinox is a glorious rendition of the ideals of innovation, conception and determination to achieve high heights of success, and is a phenomenal platform to nurture and inspire talent, while uniting a wide and diverse participation pool under a single banner.

The 2018 Programme for Dequinox is poised to exceed all expectations in keeping with its strong heritage and excelling in it's core strength as a talent platform to drive excellence.

We look forward to have an enriching experience with all the participants. We can promise it will be entertaining too!

Events

Our colossal cultural festival “Dequinox “ have done it’s part setting the ardent audience in the carnival with our celebrity of 2016 “Mohit Chauhan” . Set its sails and anchors in Westeros with the Kings “Sachin and Jigar”. With the same enthusiasm rambling around, Dequinox is soon set to disembark to a new exciting land this Dequinox 2k18. Pronites being the soul of our Fest, a soul vibrant and contagious, surprising the audience each time it sets the stage.

  • Cultural

    Verve

    Fashion is an individual statement of expression for each of us and VERVE is the avenue to express yourself. It is the chance to set yourself apart in front of an enthusiastic audience.

  • Cultural

    Panache

    The hunt for Mr. and Mrs. Dequinox. Participants go through several rounds of filtering such as written extrempore, personal interview, and may more. We test and judge participants on the overall personality and persona.

  • Music

    Raga

    Where words fail , music speaks . Captivating vocals , mellifluous keyboard , breathtaking guitars , and spell-binding drums all put together to a gob smacked evening.

  • Music

    Dropping Notes

    A platform for the euphonic voices, comprising Solo Singing and Duet Singing.

  • Dance

    Sync

    Sync is a group dance competition which brings best dancers under one roof. The group with the most excellent mix of intensity, synchronization, talent and energy will take the trophy.

  • Dance

    Double Trouble

    Manifest the best of Choreography and chemistry to triumph this Duet Dance bout. Whether you like to shake, wiggle, twirl, or bust a move, make sure you have chemistry with your partner.

  • Drama

    Nukkad Natak

    From Demonetisation of currency to Global warming bring thunder and lightning in performances that concern you, reality.Voice your thoughts in the most impassioned way through a nukkad natak that moves people to action.

  • Drama

    Stage Craft

    Life is like a drama.It's an amazing thing to be able to perform and get a reaction from people that don't know you. They don't automatically think you are wonderful because they love you, they love you because of the story you are telling on stage.
